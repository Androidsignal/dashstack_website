Antee Golf and Wager App

  • Antee Golf and Wager is a Flutter-based cross-platform mobile application designed to simplify real-time golf score tracking and wager management. Built for iOS and Android, the app allows golfers to create teams, track live scores, manage skins and side bets, and view live leaderboards‚Äîall through a fast, intuitive, and reliable mobile experience.
  • Key Features :
    • Quick Match and Team Creation : Start a golf match in seconds by creating custom teams or allowing the app to automatically randomize players for fair and balanced gameplay.
    • Real-Time Score Tracking : Enter scores per hole as you play and view instant updates across all players within your group, ensuring complete accuracy throughout the match.
    • Skins & Initial Wager Setup : Set an initial skin value per hole before the match begins and let the app automatically calculate totals as the game progresses.
    • Live Side Wagers During Match : Add or adjust side bets at any time during the round without interrupting gameplay, with all calculations handled automatically.
    • Live Leaderboards : Track player performance in real time with dynamic leaderboards and detailed match summaries at the end of each round.
    • Smart Notifications & Updates : Receive real-time match updates, score alerts, and wager confirmations via push notifications.
    • Golf News : Stay informed with the latest golf news and updates powered by Live Action Golf integration.

Features & Benefits

Antee Golf and Wager is a modern golf scoring and betting app designed for accuracy and ease of use. With Flutter at its core, it enables live score tracking, skins management, and leaderboard viewing across IOS and Android devices.

Real-Time Golf Score Tracking

Enter scores per hole and view instant updates across your group, ensuring accurate and transparent score tracking throughout the entire golf match.

Quick Team Creation & Match Setup

Create custom teams in seconds or allow the app to randomize players automatically, making match setup fast, fair, and hassle-free.

Skins & Wager Management

Set initial skin values per hole and add side wagers during live play, with automatic calculations handled seamlessly by the app.

Live Leaderboards & Match Insights

View real-time leaderboards and detailed match summaries that enhance competition and help players track performance at a glance.

Match History

Access detailed match summaries and past game history to review scores, wagers, and player performance after each round.
