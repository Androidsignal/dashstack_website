Navadiya Owner

  • Navadiya Owner is a Flutter-based accounting and business management mobile application that helps owners, managers, and accountants manage daily financial operations in one centralized system. The app works by allowing users to securely log in, create parties and sites, record income and expenses, and track approvals for every transaction. With real-time updates, role-based access, and site-wise financial tracking, Navadiya Owner ensures accurate accounting, better control, and complete transparency across Android and iOS devices.
  • Key Features:
    • Secure Login with Role-Based Dashboard : The login screen ensures authorized access for owners, managers, and accountants, while personalized dashboards display balances, site summaries, and pending actions instantly.
    • Party Creation & Bank Account Management : Screens demonstrate easy party creation with bank name, IFSC code, account number validation, and approval-based verification to maintain accurate financial records.
    • Party Approval & Status Tracking : Visual workflows show pending, approved, and rejected party requests, giving owners full control and transparency over financial entities.
    • Site & Project-Wise Accounting : Site management screens highlight independent tracking of income, expenses, and balances for each project, making the app ideal for construction and multi-site businesses.
    • Transaction Entry & Verification : Transaction screenshots display office and site RM entries with receipt uploads, approval flows, and detailed transaction history for audit-ready accounting.
    • Real-Time Financial Reports : Reporting screens provide date-wise filtering, balance summaries, and profit-expense insights, helping business owners make faster and data-driven decisions.
    • User-Permission Management : User listing screens show role-based access control, enabling secure management of managers, accountants, and staff within a single Flutter mobile application.
