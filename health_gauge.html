Features & Benefits

HealthGauge is a comprehensive health monitoring and wellness management mobile application designed to help users track vital health metrics and make informed lifestyle decisions. Built with Flutter, HealthGauge delivers a high-performance, secure, and scalable cross-platform mobile app for iOS and Android, combining real-time health tracking, smart device integration, interactive analytics, and personalized health insights into a single powerful solution for wearable Devices.

User Profile & Health Setup

HealthGauge provides a smooth onboarding experience where users securely create health profiles by adding personal details, body metrics, and preferences, enabling accurate tracking and personalized health insights.

Real-Time Health Monitoring

The app continuously tracks key health metrics such as heart rate, blood pressure, activity levels, and weight through wearable devices and smart sensors for real-time health visibility.

Smart Device & Bluetooth Integration

HealthGauge supports Bluetooth-enabled health devices and smart weight scales, ensuring seamless pairing and reliable data synchronization for accurate and automated health monitoring.

Interactive Graphs & Health Analytics

Interactive graphs, animations, and visual dashboards transform complex health data into clear insights, helping users track trends, measure progress, and make data-driven health decisions.

Smart Notifications & Reminders

Powered by FCM notifications, HealthGauge delivers timely health alerts, reminders, and activity prompts to help users stay consistent with their wellness goals.
