Challenges & Solutions

Developing a modern Insurance & Driver Rewards platform required overcoming complex real-time GPS tracking and secure telemetry data handling hurdles.

Background Location Tracking

Tracking driving behavior securely without draining the user's battery.

Implemented native background services that intelligently wake the GPS only when accelerometer data detects driving speeds.

Real-Time Driving Scores

Calculating live safety scores based on speed and braking required complex math on the device.

We shifted heavy telemetry processing to edge functions on the backend, ensuring a lightweight client application.

Claim Photo Uploads

Users in remote areas struggled to upload high-res accident photos on poor connections.

Built a robust offline-first queuing system that caches photos locally and seamlessly resumes uploads when a connection is restored.

Data Privacy Compliance

Handling sensitive vehicle and personal identity information strictly required banking-level security.

We utilized AES-256 encryption for all local storage and implemented token-based short-lived sessions for all API interactions.

Features & Benefits

Amyal is a smart Driver Rewards mobile app that tracks driving behaviour in real time to promote safer driving and fair pricing. It benefits both drivers—through rewards and insights and insurers by providing accurate, data-driven risk analysis on a single Flutter-powered platform.

Driver Onboarding

Users register quickly with a clean onboarding flow and set up their driver profile with essential details.

Real-Time Driving Tracking

The app tracks trips using live GPS data, recording distance, routes, and driving patterns with high accuracy.

Behaviour Analysis

Driving behavior such as speed, acceleration, and braking is analyzed and converted into clear performance metrics.

Rewards & Incentives

Drivers earn points and rewards based on safe driving performance, encouraging consistent and responsible behavior.

Insights & Dashboards

Visual dashboards provide drivers and insurers with actionable insights through charts, scores, and summaries.
