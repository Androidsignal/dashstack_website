123Family: Video calling for people with dementia

123Family is a communication app designed for people with dementia, limited digital skills or forgetfulness, enabling them to stay connected with family and loved ones via easy-to-use video calling. The platform focuses on simplicity: the interface is intuitive, doesn’t require training, and supports use even on older mobile devices.

  • Video Calling Built for Seniors & Memory-Impaired Users: The app emphasises body language and visual contact because for people with dementia, up to 90 % of communication can be non-verbal. By using video calling, the app allows meaningful social interaction even when digital skills are limited.
  • Full Privacy & Controlled Access: Only registered family members and friends can connect with the user, ensuring a private and safe communication circle.
  • Ultra-Simple, Training-Free Interface: 123Family's UI is designed so that even users with minimal digital experience can operate it without training. The app can even overwrite the Android home screen to avoid confusion and ensure the user stays within the app.
  • Remote Management & Support: Family members can manage and maintain the device remotely — no need for the senior user to perform updates or configurations themselves.
  • Auto-Answer Option: For users who are unable or find it difficult to press buttons, the app supports automatic call acceptance so that loved ones can reach them effortlessly.
  • Works on Older Devices: Even older smartphones or tablets can be repurposed with 123Family, making it an ideal solution for families who wish to re-use existing hardware.

Challenges & Solutions

Building an accessible app for seniors and users with cognitive impairments required us to rethink traditional UX patterns and overcome specific technical hurdles.

Zero-Learning-Curve Accessibility

Designing an interface for users with dementia requires eliminating traditional UI complexities entirely.

We developed an ultra-minimalist, high-contrast Flutter UI with oversized touch targets and a "Home-Screen Lock" feature to prevent users from accidentally exiting the app.

Unassisted Communication

Users with severe physical limitations struggle to press a button to accept incoming video calls.

We engineered a secure, permission-based "Auto-Answer" protocol allowing trusted family members to initiate calls that connect automatically without physical interaction.

Legacy Hardware Optimization

Families repurpose outdated devices for elderly relatives, which struggle with intensive video calling.

We highly optimized the Flutter rendering engine and implemented aggressive memory management to ensure lag-free video feeds even on low-RAM devices.

Secure & Private Network

Seniors with cognitive decline are highly vulnerable to spam calls, scams, and unsolicited contact.

We developed a closed-loop contact system where only pre-approved, whitelisted family members can connect with the user, ensuring a completely safe environment.

Features & Benefits

123Family keeps seniors connected through effortless video calling — combining simplicity, accessibility, and remote support for a stress-free communication experience.

Ultra-Simple Video Calling for Seniors

Designed for users with dementia or limited digital skills, 123Family offers a clean, distraction-free interface that makes video calling effortless — no training required.

Automatic Call Answering

For users who may struggle to tap buttons, incoming calls can auto-answer, ensuring family and caregivers can always reach their loved ones without confusion or missed calls.

Private, Family-Only Communication

Only approved family members and close contacts can connect, creating a safe, trusted communication environment with no external interruptions.

Remote Management & Device Support

Family members can update, configure and manage the app remotely, so seniors never need to deal with settings, updates or technical steps themselves.

Home-Screen Lock for Zero Confusion

The app can take over the device’s home screen, preventing accidental exits and keeping the user always inside a simple, controlled communication environment.

Works on Older Devices

123Family can be installed on older smartphones and tablets, allowing families to repurpose existing devices without buying new hardware.
