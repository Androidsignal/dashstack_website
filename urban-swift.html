UrbanSwift – A Complete Smart Mobility Platform

  • UrbanSwift User: UrbanSwift User is an advanced Flutter-based ecosystem designed to simplify urban logistics and personal transport through a powerful, user-centric interface. The platform uses an intuitive map interface to schedule pickups and drop-offs, track deliveries live, communicate with drivers in real time, and complete payments securely using local mobile money integrations.
  • UrbanSwift Delivery: The Delivery Partner app is built to simplify every step of the courier’s workflow. Partners can accept delivery jobs instantly, view optimized routes, communicate with customers in-app, and track earnings transparently. Designed mobile-first, it ensures fast performance even in low-network environments, supporting daily earnings summaries, route history, and efficient job tracking to boost productivity and income potential.
  • UrbanSwift Admin: UrbanSwift’s Admin Dashboard gives complete operational control. Admins can manage delivery requests, approve or suspend drivers, view user analytics, monitor KPIs like delivery time and service quality, and send real-time notifications. Built with scalability in mind, the admin panel supports data-driven decision-making and continuous improvement across the entire system.

Challenges & Solutions

Building a modern logistics and courier delivery application required flawless background GPS tracking and complex real-time routing algorithms.

Continuous Background GPS

Tracking drivers reliably without the OS terminating the app to save battery.

Engineered robust native background foreground-services that batch GPS coordinates and upload them strategically to conserve power.

Live Driver Tracking Map

Customers needed to see the driver moving smoothly on a map in real-time.

Used WebSockets to push location updates and implemented a spline-interpolation algorithm on the frontend so the car icon moves smoothly.

Dynamic Route Optimization

Drivers needed the most efficient path for multiple drop-offs in changing traffic.

Integrated complex routing APIs to calculate the optimal path instantly, updating automatically if the driver deviates from the route.

Offline Proof of Delivery

Drivers often lose signal inside large apartment complexes or basements.

Developed a local queuing system that securely stores signatures and delivery photos, automatically uploading them once signal is restored.
