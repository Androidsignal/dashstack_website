Features & Benefits

SymCheck helps providers realize cost savings by reducing the frequency of in-person visits for routine health metric monitoring. This can also reduce the burden on healthcare facilities.

Universal Accessibility

Operates on existing smartphones, eliminating the need for specialized medical devices.

Advanced Health Monitoring

Measure 4 out of 5 vital signs and nearly 50 health biomarkers through a 30-60 second face scan and AI algorithms.

Comprehensive Health Assessment

Includes 90+ clinical surveys for behavioral health issues, providing a holistic view of physical and mental well-being.

Data Security and Integration

Partners with Mastercard to ensure robust data protection and seamless integration with global financial systems.

Interoperability

Implements standardized health data interchange formats (HL7, HL7+, FHIR) for integration with national healthcare records and global health systems.

Smart Recommendation Engine

Our AI dynamically adapts to your health profile and symptom input, ensuring the recommended next steps are precisely matched to your needs.
